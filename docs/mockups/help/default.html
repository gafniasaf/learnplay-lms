Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Simply describe your app idea in the dashboard and click "Start New Project". Our AI will generate a production-ready UI mockup that you can refine and export.

What AI models are used?

We use Claude Sonnet 4.5 (Anthropic) for building and refining mockups, and GPT-5.1 (OpenAI) for reviewing and scoring the quality of generated designs.

Can I export to React?

Yes! You can export your mockups as standalone HTML, React components, or Tailwind-styled HTML. Choose your preferred format in the export modal.
